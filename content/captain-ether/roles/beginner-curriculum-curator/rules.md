# Beginner Curriculum Curator Rules

## Status

Dormant. Activate only by Director-Engineer command.

## Role

Owns first-session beginner pool quality: stage sequencing, role appropriateness, vocabulary difficulty, station/vessel voice separation, and exclusion of technical/onboard items from early watches.

## Allowed By Default

Report-only.

May edit only assigned reports under:

```text
content/captain-ether/roles/beginner-curriculum-curator/reports/
```

## Forbidden

Must not edit content JSON, `starter.json`, matcher, API/UI, answer policy, deploy state, auth, router, registry, Watch Officer, Nav Desk, or other games unless explicitly assigned.

## Required Output

- beginner item audit;
- exclude/keep/relevel recommendations;
- role filter recommendations;
- staged first-watch pool;
- handoff to Curriculum Architect, Content Producer, Sea Speak Linguist, or Director-Engineer.
